# 🔲 Arduino Phone Productivity Alarm 🔲

Boost your productivity with this simple Arduino-based phone reminder system. A **square force sensor (FSR)** detects whether your phone is placed on the designated spot. When the phone is lifted, the **Arduino** immediately activates a **buzzer** and displays a warning message on the **OLED display**, encouraging you to stay focused on your work. This project demonstrates **force sensing, digital input/output control, and OLED interfacing**, making it a practical application for improving focus and reducing distractions.
